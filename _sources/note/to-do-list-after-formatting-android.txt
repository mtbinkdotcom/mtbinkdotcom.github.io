Some settings
-------------

#.  Untick some Google account sync service:

    #.  **Settings > Cloud and accounts > Accounts > Google**.
    #.  Then click your email address.
    #.  **Untick** the following (they are not important to me):

        *   Sync Contacts
        *   Sync Drive
        *   Sync Google Fit Data
        *   Sync Google Play Movies & TV
        *   Sync Google Play Music
        *   Sync People details
        
        and **tick** all others.
    #.  Need to re-do for another email address (if any). My another account
        needs just these **ticked**:
        
        *   Sync Gmail

#.  Use Google DNS:

    #.  **Settings > Connections > Wi-Fi**
    #.  Tap and hold the current Wi-Fi networks and then choose 
        **Manage network settings**
    #.  **Tick** the **Show advanced options**
    #.  IP settings: **Static**
    #.  IP address: **192.168.IP**, replace **IP** accordingly.
    #.  DNS 1: **8.8.8.8**
    #.  DNS 2: **8.8.4.4**

#.  Check that the device can run Android Studio development. 
    Enable developer option and USB debugging mode:

    #.  **Settings > About phone > Software information**
    #.  Tap **Build number** several times (maybe 7) and then
        **Developer options** should appears in **Settings**.
    #.  Enable **Developer options** first, then 
        **Settings > Developer options > Debugging** and tick **USB debugging**.

#.  Go to `Android Device Manager <https://www.google.com/android/devicemanager>`_, 
    then locate the device and click **Enable Lock and Erase**.

#.  Setup **ringtone** and **notification** in **Settings > Sounds and vibration**.

#.  Change **wallpaper** and **lockscreen** wallpaper.
    
#.  Create my personal folders:

    *   **Do not** create the folders through PC, it will hang.
    *   Create everything initially then simply copy everything to
        device
    *   My setup:

        *   **a/d**
        *   **a/m**
        *   **a/smartphone**
        *   **a/transit**
       
#.  Install all of the :doc:`important Android apps </software-registry/android>`.
