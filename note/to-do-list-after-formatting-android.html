11.1. Some settings¶

  1. Untick some Google account sync service:

    1. Settings > Cloud and accounts > Accounts > Google.

    2. Then click your email address.

    3. Untick the following (they are not important to me):

      • Sync Contacts
      • Sync Drive
      • Sync Google Fit Data
      • Sync Google Play Movies & TV
      • Sync Google Play Music
      • Sync People details

      and tick all others.

    4. Need to re-do for another email address (if any). My another account needs just these ticked:

      • Sync Gmail

  2. Use Google DNS:

    1. Settings > Connections > Wi-Fi
    2. Tap and hold the current Wi-Fi networks and then choose Manage network settings
    3. Tick the Show advanced options
    4. IP settings: Static
    5. IP address: 192.168.IP, replace IP accordingly.
    6. DNS 1: 8.8.8.8
    7. DNS 2: 8.8.4.4

  3. Check that the device can run Android Studio development. Enable developer option and USB debugging mode:

    1. Settings > About phone > Software information
    2. Tap Build number several times (maybe 7) and then Developer options should appears in Settings.
    3. Enable Developer options first, then Settings > Developer options > Debugging and tick USB debugging.

  4. Go to Android Device Manager, then locate the device and click Enable Lock and Erase.

  5. Setup ringtone and notification in Settings > Sounds and vibration.

  6. Change wallpaper and lockscreen wallpaper.

  7. Create my personal folders:

    • Do not create the folders through PC, it will hang.
    • Create everything initially then simply copy everything to device
    • My setup:
      • a/d
      • a/m
      • a/smartphone
      • a/transit

  8. Install all of the important Android apps.
